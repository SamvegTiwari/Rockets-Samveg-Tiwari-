Rockets








Rockets are vehicles that acquire thrust from their engines to launch them up into the air/space to find out about the universe and beyond.
They work due to newtons law of motion that is Everything has an equal and opposite reaction. In a rocket, burning fuel gives a push to the frontal area of the rocket launching it forward. Thus, creates an equal and opposite force on the exhaust backward. Leading the rocket to go up in the air.
They are very helpful in carrying humans to space and also in providing data, although they pollute a lot because they release large quantities of gas from the exhaust and while the payload is in space, the rest falls down and goes waste in the ocean. Which pollutes water and affects marine life..
Some companies are trying to make more eco-friendly rockets.
